Jonathan's Steak Recipe

Description

The original recipe to Jonathan's famous savory steak! This recipe will show you how to make Jonathan's steak with easy instructions so you can get a good meal!

Ingredients

  • New York Strip Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Rosemary

Steps to Creating Steak

  1. Pat steaks dry to prevent soggy steaks
  2. Generously season steaks with salt and pepper on all sides
  3. On a pan add olive oil and heat on high
  4. Once pan is hot, add steak and press down on side to create a golden brown crust
  5. Add butter and rosemary into pan after one side is finished cooking
  6. Flip and continue to sear until desired wellness
  7. Let steak sit for 2 minutes then enjoy
